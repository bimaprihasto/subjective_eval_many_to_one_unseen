Webpage for the purpose of evaluating the mean opinion score (MOS)

Many-to-one voice conversion (unseen)

比馬特

DLMSL, CSIE, NCU-Taiwan

There are 2 types of measurement:

  1. Naturalness: measures speech produced similar to a human voice (not a robotic voice)
  2. Speaker similarity: measures the similarity based on the character of the voice with the conversion target (this similarity is not related to the sentence that is spoken)
There are 4 methods (A, B, C, and D) that we put randomly to avoid tagging and to be fair. Each consists of 2 baselines and 2 proposed methods.
